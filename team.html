Solar People

To stay consistent with our motto of creating not just solar homes, but solar people, we’ve formed a team of interaction designers out of Stanford’s Hasso Plattner Institute of Design to re- engineer how homeowners interact with basic daily tasks, such as using the bathroom or turning on a light which. The process of developing these devices will begin with need-finding missions across campus, speaking with local homeowners about daily behavior within the home, and then prototyping and developing devices to gently educate homeowners about hoe to live more sustainably.
