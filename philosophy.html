Our Mission

Our solar house is a global statement in energy-efficient construction. We will use groundbreaking solar technology to make it attractive, affordable, and easy to live in.

Solar Decathlon is a two-year project sponsored by the U.S. Department of Energy to promote solar technology and energy-efficient construction. In 2013, Stanford placed fifth overall.

Philosophy

We are an award-winning technology product design and development company. We define next-generation products and user experiences. This is our quest for mastery. We focus those products and experiences on preferable outcomes for society. biggest design challenge: creating a world-class design company. It all rests upon attracting the best designers, strategists, engineers, and researchers in the world. Then comes an effort to continually craft an environment.

Our Design Plans

Real-Time Updates

Our team is ideating a system of sensors that collect real-time data, presenting it through visual display and installed artwork throughout the house. These panels around the home would allow the homeowners to adjust temperatures, lighting, and other critical home controls, while receiving feed-back on their energy and water consumption.

Solar People

Finally, to stay consistent with our motto of creating not just solar homes, but solar people, we’ve formed a team of interaction designers out of Stanford’s Hasso Plattner Institute of Design to re- engineer how homeowners interact with basic daily tasks, such as using the bathroom or turning on a light which. The process of developing these devices will begin with need-finding missions across campus, speaking with local homeowners about daily behavior within the home, and then prototyping and developing devices to gently educate homeowners about hoe to live more sustainably.
