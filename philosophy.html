Our Mission

Our solar house is a global statement in energy-efficient construction. We will use groundbreaking solar technology to make it attractive, affordable, and easy to live in.

Solar Decathlon 2015

Solar Decathlon is a biennial project sponsored by the U.S. Department of Energy to promote solar technology and energy-efficient construction. In 2013, Stanford placed fifth overall.


Visit their website

Philosophy

The Stanford team is unique in its user-centered design methodology. We conduct user interviews, create personas, and partner with real people to check all our design decisions against data rather than assumptions. We are the only team to partner with an end-user who will live in the house after the competition and who we partner with to make an enjoyable living experience.

Our design plans

Real-Time Updates

Our team is ideating a system of sensors that collect real-time data, presenting it through visual display and installed artwork throughout the house. These panels around the home would allow the homeowners to adjust temperatures, lighting, and other critical home controls. This feedback would encourage efficient behavior and help change wasteful habits.
